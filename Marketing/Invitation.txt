Dear fellow students,

Do you want to learn how to hack or challenge your skills in IT security?
There is good news, Neuland CTF returns this semester.

Capture the Flag (CTF) is a competition that allows you to hack legally and solve exciting challenges.
For each correct challenge there is a flag that will grant you points and a place on the scoreboard.
No previous knowledge is required, the CTF contains tasks in different categories and levels of difficulty.

The event will take place on December 9rd, 2023 at the Technische Hochschule Ingolstadt in the room G215.

Time schedule:
- 10:30 a.m. - 11:00 a.m. Opening and introduction
- 11:00 a.m. - 07:00 p.m. CTF competition time
- 07:00 p.m. - 09:00 p.m. Get together

Please note that participation is only possible after registration at https://ctf.neuland-ingolstadt.de/!
You can participate alone or with a team of up to 3 people.

Prices:
1st team: 250 €
2nd team: 150 €
3rd team: 100 €
4th - 5th team: 15 € voucher for each team member

In addition to prizes for the best participants, we will provide drinks, lunch and dinner as well as T-shirts and stickers for all participants.
